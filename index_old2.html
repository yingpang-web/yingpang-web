I am Ying Pang (庞颖 in Chinese), a final-year PhD student in Computer Science Department at University of Otago. Currently, I am studying in Systems Research Group (SRG) and supervised by Haibo Zhang, Jeremiah D. Deng, and Lizhi Peng (external).

My research primarily focuses on distributed optimization, federated learning, robust learning with label noise, and imbalanced learning. Currently, I am working on designing, analyzing, and evaluating collaborative learning frameworks that address real-world constraints, including heterogeneity, communication issues, privacy concerns, interpretability, and robustness to label noise.
